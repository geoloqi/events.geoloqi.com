Foursquare API Hackathon (San Francisco)

Saturday, January 5, 2013 from 9am–6pm
Foursquare SF
363 Clementina St
San Francisco, CA 94103, US (map)

Website

http://www.meetup.com/foursquare-API/San-Francisco-CA/828942/

Description

On January 5th, we’ll open up our NYC & SF offices for a connected apps hackathon of epic proportions. And just like last year, we’re inviting developers all around the world to join us by banding together in their hometowns and hacking up some awesome connected apps of their own. We’ll have prizes, swag, and (naturally) global glory for the best foursquare hacks, no matter where in the world they come from.
